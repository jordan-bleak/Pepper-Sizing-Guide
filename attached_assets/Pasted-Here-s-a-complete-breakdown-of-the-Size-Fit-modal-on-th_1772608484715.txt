Here's a complete breakdown of the "Size & Fit" modal on the Pepper product page. It has four tabs:
Tab 1: Size Chart
This is the main reference chart. It's a 2D grid that cross-references two body measurements to find your bra size.
How to read it:

Columns (top axis) = Bust measurement in ranges (e.g., 30–32", 32–33", up to 40–41")
Rows (left axis) = Under-bust measurement in ranges (e.g., 25–26", 27–28", up to 35–36")
The intersection cell shows your bra size (e.g., 34A) and the corresponding alpha size (XXS, XS, S, M, L, XL)
Unit toggle: You can switch between IN (inches) and CM (centimeters). In CM mode, an additional size row appears (42AA/42A/42B at 93–97 cm under-bust).
Color coding: Cells are shaded in light blue, with slightly darker blue highlighting used for certain size groups (the A-cup column appears most prominently highlighted, reflecting Pepper's AA–B cup specialty).
The full size range covered:
| Under-bust | Sizes available |
|---|---|
| 25–26" | 30A (XXS), 30B (XXS) |
| 27–28" | 32AA (XS), 32A (XS), 32B (XS) |
| 29–30" | 34AA (S), 34A (S), 34B (S) |

| 33–34" | 38AA (L), 38A (L), 38B (L) |
| 35–36" | 40AA (XL), 40A (XL), 40B (XL) |
Right-side panel (visible in all tabs) contains three advisory sections:
HOW TO MEASURE — instructs to measure around the ribcage (under-bust) on exhale for band size, and around the fullest part of the bust for cup size. Recommends sizing up if between cup sizes. Contact: pepsquad@wearpepper.com.
FIND YOUR SISTER SIZE — explains that moving vertically on the chart finds sister sizes with the same cup volume (e.g., 30A and 32AA are sister sizes).
PEPPER FIT TIPS — states bras are designed for AA–B cups. Additional tips: go true-to-size for maximum lift; size up the band and down the cup for a relaxed fit; fuller-B cups are pointed to specific styles (Signature All You, Limitless Wirefree Scoop Bra, Signature Unlined Demi Bra).


Tab 2: Size Calculator
A calculator tool that takes your raw measurements and outputs your recommended size.
How it works:

IN mode: Two inputs — Band Size (your ribcage/under-bust measurement, e.g., 34.5") and Bust Size (your full bust measurement, e.g., 34.5")
CM mode: Two inputs — Underbust Size (e.g., 60 cm) and Bust Size (e.g., 73 cm)
After clicking "GET MY SIZE", the result appears below as "Your recommended size is: [size]", along with a "Shop Bras" link
If measurements fall outside the available range, it displays: "We currently do not offer this size. Please double check your measurements or reach out to our fit experts."


Tab 3: How to Measure
A visual step-by-step measurement guide, combining a video and illustrated instructions.
Video: A branded Pepper video titled "Finding Your Bra Size" (playable within the modal).
Text intro: Instructs users to wear a lightly lined bra (no push-ups or compression bras) and use a soft measuring tape (or a string + ruler).
Two illustrated steps, each with a photo:

Underbust Measurement — Wrap the tape around your ribcage just beneath your bust, keeping it parallel to the floor and level around your back. Exhale and take the measurement.
Bust Measurement — Wrap the tape around the fullest part of your bust, again keeping it parallel to the floor. Make sure the tape is snug but not too tight.
